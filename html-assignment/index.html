About Me

My name is Sean Emmerson. I'm from Ottawa, I'm 19 years old and a student at Algonquin College. In my spare time, I enjoy taking pictures and making music. In fact, I recently graduated from the MIA program here at Algonquin with Dean's Honours. My end goal after this program is to secure a job in graphic or web design, since I'm fond of both.

I hope to learn a lot from this course, and I hope to incorporate web development and elements of design thinking into my everyday hobbies. I'm excited to implement new things I learn into my art and to solve problems in the real and virtual world. Oh yeah, gotta add that superscript somewhere, right?
